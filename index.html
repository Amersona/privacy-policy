Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Agrovoce ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you interact with our automated content posting services on LinkedIn and other social media platforms.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Property Listing Data: Information about real estate listings including property details, images, prices, locations, and descriptions.
  • Usage Data: Information about how our automated systems interact with social media APIs to post content.
  • API Tokens: Secure authentication tokens required to post content to social media platforms (stored securely and never shared).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Automatically generate and post real estate content to social media platforms
  • Improve the quality and relevance of automated posts
  • Maintain and secure our automation workflows
  • Comply with legal obligations and platform API terms of service

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Social Media Platforms: LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube as required to post content through their APIs
  • Service Providers: Third-party tools like n8n for workflow automation and AI services for content generation
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including encrypted API token storage, secure workflow configurations, and access controls.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. API tokens are rotated regularly for security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
